# FactionValues

The FactionValues quiz measures where you stand across seven axes of revolutionary and
political strategy, then matches you to the faction or ideology closest to your answers:

- **Ground** &mdash; Insurrection vs. Coup D'&Eacute;tat
- **Intelligence** &mdash; Domestic vs. Foreign
- **Aid** &mdash; Mutual Aid vs. State Aid
- **Enforcement** &mdash; Restorative vs. Punitive
- **Management** &mdash; Decentralized vs. Centralized
- **Energy** &mdash; Renewable vs. Extractive
- **Proletariat** &mdash; Labor vs. Capital

Questions live in `questions.js`, faction/ideology reference points live in `ideologies.js`,
and the axis titles/colors/pole names are configured in `results.html`. Add or edit a
question in `questions.js` and the quiz, scoring, and results page pick it up automatically
&mdash; no other files need to change.
