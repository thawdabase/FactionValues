What is FactionValues?

FactionValues is a quiz that measures where you stand across seven axes of revolutionary and political strategy — from how change should be won, to who should hold power once it is. Answer honestly and find out which of the many factions your values line up with. There are questions in the test.

What are the 7 axes?

Each question moves you along one of seven axes. Here's what each pole means:

INSURRECTION

Those who lean Insurrection believe lasting change comes from below: general strikes, mass demonstrations, and popular uprisings. They trust the crowd in the street over the cadre in the barracks.

GROUND

COUP D'ÉTAT

Those who lean Coup D'État believe a small, disciplined group seizing key institutions is faster and more reliable than waiting on a spontaneous popular revolt.

DOMESTIC

Those who lean Domestic believe resources and attention are best spent solving problems at home first, building strong local institutions before looking abroad.

INTELLIGENCE

FOREIGN

Those who lean Foreign are internationalist, valuing solidarity, cooperation, and coordination across borders over a purely domestic focus.

MUTUAL AID

Those who lean Mutual Aid trust communities to look after each other directly — through informal, peer-to-peer networks — over waiting on institutional bureaucracy.

AID

STATE AID

Those who lean State Aid trust a well-funded, centrally administered welfare system to reach more people, more reliably, than informal charity ever could.

RESTORATIVE

Those who lean Restorative believe justice should focus on repairing harm and reintegrating offenders into society, not on punishment for its own sake.

ENFORCEMENT

PUNITIVE

Those who lean Punitive believe strict, certain punishment is the best deterrent to crime, and that some acts deserve harsh consequences regardless of circumstance.

DECENTRALIZED

Those who lean Decentralized believe decisions are best made locally, by those closest to the problem, even at the cost of some efficiency.

MANAGEMENT

CENTRALIZED

Those who lean Centralized believe a strong central authority is necessary to coordinate large-scale efforts and respond effectively to complex crises.

RENEWABLE

Those who lean Renewable support a rapid transition to solar, wind, and other clean energy, even at some short-term economic cost.

ENERGY

EXTRACTIVE

Those who lean Extractive believe oil, gas, and mining remain essential to a functioning economy, and that cutting them off too quickly would do more harm than good.

LABOR

Those who lean Labor believe workers should control the enterprises they work in, and that profits rightly belong to the people who create them.

PROLETARIAT

CAPITAL

Those who lean Capital believe investment and ownership drive innovation, and that protecting investors and business owners is key to a healthy economy.


What's the "Closest Match" mean at the bottom of the results?

In addition to scoring you on the seven axes above, the quiz also attempts to match you to a political ideology or faction. This is a work in progress and is much less accurate than the axes themselves, so don't take it too seriously. If you disagree with your assigned faction, open an issue on GitHub with your scores, matched faction, and preferred faction, and we'll look into adjusting the system. Thanks!
